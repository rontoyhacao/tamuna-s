About Tamuna's

Tamuna's Fruit Fusion Cakes is known for its tasty fruit cakes that aim to satisfy dessert cravings of everyone who looks for a unique taste in a cake.

Tamuna's Fruit Fusion Cakes is a small business based in San Pablo City, Laguna which started in early 2020 by Miss Anne Pauline Matanguihan.
